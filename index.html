About
Me

Hello and welcome to my little corner of the Internet ! My name is Isha and I am an emerging web developer currently embarking on my Fullstack journey with Carleton and edX bootcamps. Prior to this, my academic training was in biochemistry with practical experience in developing prototype nano-carriers with Health Canada. I owe great thanks and respect to the instructors and mentors that guided me into the quizzical and methodical thinking that is required for scientific research and discovery. With time I came to realise that working at the bench was not the passion that I would wish to continue to pursue. The exploding and exciting field of software and web development enticed me into looking at what options were available to me. While I am excited to learn this new skill, I am avidly curious of how to bridge the gap between this field and that of science. With my practical experience in scientific methods, experiment design, and technical report reading and writing, I hope to find the medium that can express the portfolio I have built.
